We Are DUT Studio

DUT Studio was founded in 2021 to develop, publish, and support the most player-focused games. In 2021, we released our debut title The Death - Than Trung. We’re continuing to evolve the game while delivering new experiences to players.

Founded by 3 people who are all famous streamers in the gaming community. DUT Studio is headquartered in Ha Noi
